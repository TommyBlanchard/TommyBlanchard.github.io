About

Sat 28 January 2017

Hello! This is my (Tommy Blanchard's) personal site. This site is a place for me to showcase some of my data science projects (from small learning projects to data exploration projects to publications). It's also home to my personal blog, where I plan to write about academia-vs-industry, data science, science, and whatever else strikes my fancy.

I'm currently a data scientist working at Fresenius Medical Care on the Integrated Care Analytics team. In a past life I was an academic neuroscientist. I was a postdoc at Harvard, doing research in the Computational Cognitive Neuroscience lab. I did my Bachelor's in computer science at the University of Waterloo, and got my PhD in Brain and Cognitive Sciences at the University of Rochester.
